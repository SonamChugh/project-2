PROCESS

Invest In Success, Cut The Rest

With our highly-skilled marketing science team, we are able to provide technically complex models and simulators as quickly and as accurately as possible to help our clients:

  • Find more ways to minimize costs and gain higher margins
  • Build an optimal strategy to create a lean and mean business
  • Improve operational performance and efficiency
  • Drive more revenue to their business

The Challenge:Our client was burdened by a broad and complex product line, with many indistinguishable products in a shrinking market with declining revenue

Our Solution:A comprehensive market model that established which products to keep, which products to develop, and which products to kill. Final market configuration reduced the number of existing product from 28 to 16, while adding 3 new products. Top-line revenues increased by $300M with significant savings in process cost that included engineering, manufacturing, distribution, and ongoing product support.
