RETENTION

Customer Retention And Predictive Modeling

How do you deal with customer attrition when your loss looks like it’s driving your competitor’s gain? To understand the reasons behind attrition, we have created a multi-step process that aims to:

  • Identify customer churn risk signals
  • Understand the brand and market better
  • Reduce the flow of customers from the brand to their competitors
  • Apply strategies that will effectively keep customer attrition rate low
  • Gain insights that will help maintain customer relationships

The Challenge:Retention has cost this company a $53 million dollar problem, where $28 million are accounted for lost accounts and $25 million in negotiated concessions. This technology company wanted to know what are the customers' perceptions of their brand and more exactly, why are they leaving for a different provider. It is unclear how introducing new features will make users see a reason for them to pay. The company has responded by doing quarterly product launches which are 3-4 times more often than their competition.

Our Solution:

Avidelo's solution struck in two dimensions, identifying key attrition drivers and identifying at risk customer relationships. Data-driven solutions quickly and effectively uncover attrition drivers and 'at risk' accounts allowing decision makers to develop targeted solutions. Predictive networks evaluate client accounts and account indicators and unnecessary concessions while simultaneously identifying healthy high cohesion sectors for growth, solving problems while building customer loyalty in the long run.
